Not Just a Class, an Experience

At Sophy’s Taste, we believe good food is an art
but great food is teachable. Our trainings are not just about recipes.
They’re about passing down a craft,
the kind that turns everyday ingredients into something unforgettable.

Whether you’re a curious beginner, a stay-at-home parent looking to earn,
or a rising caterer aiming to master small chops and pastries
you belong here.

What You’ll Learn:

  • Master samosas, spring rolls &
    puff-puff with the perfect crunch
  • Bake soft, moist cakes and cupcakes
    with pro-level presentation
  • Dough work: meat pies, filled/glazed
    doughnuts, and more
  • Learn pricing, portioning, packaging
    & hygiene essentials
  • How to start selling confidently
    with what you already have

Who It’s For

  • Absolute beginners & hobby cooks
  • Stay-at-home parents
  • Business owners looking to improve their chops
  • Anyone who wants to turn cooking into income
