At Sophy’s taste, we don’t just make food, we create joyful moments, one bite at a time.

Born out of love for hospitality and a flair for flavor, Sophy’s Taste was founded to bring
premium small chops, beautiful cakes, and delightful baked treats to events, homes,
and happy tables across Nigeria. Every item from our golden samosas to our smooth
buttercream cakes is crafted with care, creativity, and quality ingredients.

We believe that food should be an experience; elegant, memorable, and made with heart.

Our Mission

To serve exceptional small chops and baked goods with a personal touch, combining great taste,
beautiful presentation, and prompt service, while inspiring the next generation of foodpreneurs through hands-on training.

Our Vision

To become Nigeria’s most trusted small chops and baking brand,known for flavor, finesse, and a warm customer experience.

Where to Find Us

Based in Zaria, Kaduna, Nigeria, we cater to events, parties, and cravings within and outside the city.
Orders can be placed online, and deliveries are available on request.
