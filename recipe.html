Red Velvet Cupcakes

Soft, rich, and topped with dreamy whipped cream, this is the cupcake that keeps customers
coming back. Perfect for birthdays, small chops trays, or late-night sweet cravings.

Ingredients:

For the cupcakes:
  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tbsp cocoa powder
  • ½ tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 large eggr
  • ¾ cup vegetable oil
  • ½ cup buttermilk
  • 1 tbsp red food coloring
  • 1 tsp white vinegar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
For the whipped cream topping:
  • 1 cup heavy cream (cold)
  • 2 tbsp powdered sugar
  • ½ tsp vanilla extract
  • Glacé cherries and red velvet crumbs
    (for garnish)

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 175°C (350°F) and line a cupcake pan with wrappers.
  2. In one bowl, mix all dry ingredients: flour, cocoa powder, sugar, salt, and baking soda.
  3. In another bowl, whisk egg, oil, buttermilk, vinegar, vanilla, and food coloring.
  4. Combine wet and dry ingredients until just mixed — don’t overbeat.
  5. Scoop into cupcake liners and bake for 18–20 mins or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  6. Let cool completely before frosting.
  7. For the topping, whip heavy cream with powdered sugar and vanilla until stiff peaks form.
  8. Pipe or spoon over cooled cupcakes, and garnish with cherry and red velvet crumbs.

Why We Love It:

  • Light and moist
  • Bold red color makes it a crowd favorite
  • That whipped topping? Heavenly
